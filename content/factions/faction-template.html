The Endmarch

The Red Faction is a military-driven society, led by the fierce and relentless Infernal General. They believe in conquest, war, and the right to dominate.

Strengths

  • Superior military power
  • Fast expansion
  • Ability to raid and plunder

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable to diplomacy and long-term strategies
  • Reliant on military might
